Trust & Accreditation

Recognized & Certified

Al-Khidmat Hospital Sahiwal is the first private hospital in Sahiwal to receive these prestigious certifications.

ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Valid February 2024 – November 2026. International standard for quality management systems — covering Emergency Services, Diagnostic Lab, Radiology, Gynae & Obs, General & Orthopedic Surgery, Medicine, Pediatrics, NICU, Pharmacy, Dialysis, Physiotherapy, and Cath Lab.

First in Sahiwal — PHC Regular License

Punjab Healthcare Commission Regular License No. RL/No. 2083236578. The 1st private hospital in Sahiwal to achieve this status — empaneled under the Sehat Sahulat Program (SSP) for free treatment of eligible patients.
